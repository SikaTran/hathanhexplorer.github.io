Ha Noi Culture

Ha Noi is one of the largest cultural centers in Viet Nam. In the city area, many libraries, museums, theaters as well as historical sites, landcapes are concentrated. The performing arts activites in Ha Noi also take place very likely and diverse.

Cultural Palace

The Palace of Cutural is the venue for both performing arts activities and cultural activities for the community such as art classes, cutural contests. Hanoi's largest cultural palace is the Hanoi Friendship Cultural Palace (also known as Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Cultural Palace). In addition, Hanoi also has some other cultural palaces such as Children's Cutural Palace and Palace of youth culture

Theater

The largest theater in Hanoi is the Hanoi Opera House (formerly known as the West Theater, which hosts concerts or large concerts. In addition, the city also has many performing arts venues in Hanoi:

  • Central Cheo Theater
  • Central puppet theater
  • Youth Theater
  • Hanoi Tuong Theater
  • Worker Theater
  • Hong Ha Cinema
