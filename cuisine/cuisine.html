Food you don't want to skip when you visit Ha Noi

"Source: cookpad.com"

"Bun dau mam tom" is one of the famous and local's favorite dishes. It can be classfied as a street food. Bun dau mam tom has three main ingredients: vermicelli noodle, fried tofu, and shrimp paste Besides, you can find meat, pig's tripes featuring with vesgetable such as Latuca sativa, Elsholtzia cristata, cucumber, etc. The characteristic of Bun dau mam tom is shrim paste with a strong smell combining to a little sour taste of lemon or kumquat with the spice from the chili. All combines togther that make a special food you can easily be addicted.
